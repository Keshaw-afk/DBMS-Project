Relational Schema Summary:
-------------------------
1. Department: Master hub for institutional organization.
2. Student/Faculty: Core human entities linked to Departments.
3. Course: Academic offerings linked to Faculty and Dept.
4. Enrollment: Central transaction entity connecting Students to Courses.
5. Grade/Attendance/Alerts: Performance metrics linked to Enrollments.
6. FeePayment: Financial tracking per student/semester.
7. AuditLog: System-wide integrity and event tracking.
